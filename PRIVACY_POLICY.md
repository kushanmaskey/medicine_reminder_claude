# Privacy Policy — My Medical Wallet

**Last updated: May 2026**

## Overview

My Medical Wallet ("the App") is a personal health tracking app. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

---

## What Data We Collect

| Data | Why |
|---|---|
| Email address | To create and authenticate your account |
| Password (hashed) | Secure login — we never store plain-text passwords |
| Health vitals (blood pressure, blood sugar, weight, cholesterol) | Core app functionality — to track and display your health data |
| Medications and prescriptions | To help you manage and track your medications |
| Appointments | To track your medical appointments |
| Doctor information | To store your healthcare provider details |
| Activity records | To log personal health activities |
| Gender / sex | To personalise relevant health tracking (e.g. monthly vitals) |
| Notifications preferences | To send medication and appointment reminders |

---

## How We Store Your Data

All data is stored securely using **Supabase** (a cloud database provider). Your data is:

- Encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Protected by Row Level Security — only **you** can access your own data
- Never sold to third parties
- Never used for advertising

---

## Health Information Disclaimer

The health recommendations shown in this App are **for informational purposes only** and are **not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment**. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider before making any health decisions.

---

## Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

- **Supabase** (supabase.com) — database and authentication
- **MedlinePlus** (medlineplus.gov) — external health information links (opened in browser)
- **NPI Registry** (npiregistry.cms.hhs.gov) — public doctor lookup (read-only, no data sent)

---

## Notifications

With your permission, the App sends local notifications for medication reminders and appointments. These are processed entirely on your device — no notification content is sent to any server.

---

## Your Rights

You can:

- **View** all your data inside the App at any time
- **Delete** any entry (vitals, medications, appointments, doctors) from within the App
- **Delete your account** — contact us and we will remove all your data from Supabase within 30 days

---

## Children

This App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.
