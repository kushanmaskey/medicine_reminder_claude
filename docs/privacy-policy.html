Privacy Policy

App name: My Medical Wallet
Package: com.mymedicalwallet.app
Last updated: July 21, 2026

1. Information We Collect

My Medical Wallet collects only the information you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Your name and email address (for account creation)
  • Medication names, dosages, and schedules you enter
  • Health vitals you choose to record (blood pressure, weight, blood sugar, etc.)
  • Doctor and insurance information you enter
  • Appointment details you add

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Send you medication reminder notifications
  • Display your health information within the app
  • Sync your data securely across your devices via Supabase

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal health information with third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, which uses industry-standard encryption. We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorised access.

4. Third-Party Services

My Medical Wallet uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — secure cloud database and authentication
  • RevenueCat — subscription and in-app purchase management

5. Permissions

The app may request the following device permissions:

  • Notifications — to send medication reminders
  • Biometric authentication — for secure login (fingerprint/face)
  • Camera / Photo library — to add profile photos

6. Children's Privacy

My Medical Wallet is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
